## 2. CRITICAL GAPS

### 🚨 Government Schemes Integration (Major Gap)
**Missing:** Comprehensive tracking of Ireland's three main homebuyer schemes:

1. **Help to Buy Scheme** - Up to €30,000 rebate for new builds (first-time buyers only)
2. **First Home Scheme** - Up to 30% shared equity (20% if using HTB) for new builds
3. **Local Authority Home Loan** - Government mortgages up to 90% LTV with increased limits in 2025

**Impact:** Buyers could miss out on €50,000+ in government support. These schemes have complex eligibility rules and property price ceilings that vary by county.

### 🚨 Legal Process Tracking (Critical Gap)
**Missing:** Conveyancing milestone tracking with solicitor integration
- Contract review and negotiation stages
- Title investigation and searches
- Family Home Declaration requirements
- Completion timeline management (typically 2-3 months)

**Impact:** Legal delays are the #1 cause of sale failures in Ireland. No visibility into this process leaves buyers blind to critical risks.

### 🚨 Stamp Duty Calculator (Financial Gap)
**Missing:** Real-time stamp duty calculations based on:
- Property type (new vs second-hand)
- Purchase price
- Buyer status (first-time vs subsequent)
- Bulk purchase rules (15% rate for 10+ units)

**Current rates:** 1% up to €1M, 2% above €1M (calculated on net price for new builds excluding 13.5% VAT)

### 🚨 Defective Blocks Risk Assessment (Building Safety Gap)
**Missing:** Systematic checking for pyrite/mica risks beyond basic 2001-2013 date range
- County-specific risk mapping (Donegal, Mayo worst affected)
- Quarry source tracking for blocks
- Remediation scheme eligibility checking (€462,000 max grant increased in 2024)

## 5. GOVERNMENT SCHEMES & GRANTS GAPS

### 🏛️ Missing Scheme Integration

**Help to Buy Scheme (2025 Rules):**
- Eligibility: First-time buyers only, new builds only
- Amount: Up to €30,000 (20% of purchase price, max)
- Property limits: €500,000 (most areas), €600,000 (Dublin/Cork)
- **App Gap:** No eligibility checker or rebate calculator

**First Home Scheme (Shared Equity):**
- Eligibility: First-time buyers, new builds, income limits apply
- Amount: Up to 30% equity (20% if using HTB)
- Property limits: Vary by county (€450,000-€500,000 range)
- **App Gap:** No shared equity tracking or exit strategy planning

**Local Authority Home Loan (2025 Updates):**
- Eligibility: First-time buyers who can't get commercial mortgages
- Limits: Increased significantly in 2025 (up to €415,000 in Dublin area)
- Income limits: €80,000 (single), €85,000 (joint)
- **App Gap:** No alternative mortgage option tracking

**SEAI Grants (Enhanced 2025):**
- Individual grants: Up to €30,000+ for energy upgrades
- Warmer Homes: Fully funded upgrades for qualifying households
- Heat pump grants: €6,500 + €4,000 renewable bonus
- **App Gap:** Limited integration beyond basic mention in AI estimates
